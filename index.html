Which player's turn is it?
—
Objective: Score more points than your opponent.

Game Play:
1) The public number is the sum of the two white dice.
2) All players may cross off
the public number in at most one colour per turn.
3) On their turn, a player may also select at most one number that is the sum of a white die and a coloured die.
4) Players may not cross off a number in a row that is to the left of a number they have already crossed off.

Penalties: A player is given a penalty if they
do not select at least one number on their turn.

Lock Row: To lock a row, the player must have at least 6 numbers, including the right-most,
crossed off to lock the row.

End of Game: The game ends in one of two ways: a player gets four strikeouts OR any two rows are locked.
